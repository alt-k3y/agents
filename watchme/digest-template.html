FERC orders six grid operators to fast-track power for AI data centers
FERC issued Section 206 "show cause" orders on June 18 to every US regional grid operator except Texas, demanding faster interconnection for large loads · Chair Laura Swett called AI grid access a "national priority." The orders skip the years-long rulemaking process, a direct answer to Microsoft adding 4 GW in 18 months and CoreWeave targeting 1.7 GW by year-end. Who should worry: ratepayers, since who funds the grid upgrades is the unanswered question.
